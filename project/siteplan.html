EcoCart - Site Plan

Site Name

EcoCart – a small online shop for eco-friendly home goods.

This name reflects our commitment to eco-friendly shopping and sustainability.

Site Purpose

EcoCart aims to provide an easy-to-use platform for shopping sustainable home products, helping customers reduce waste and make environmentally conscious choices.

Visitor Scenarios

  • Where can I find reusable kitchen products that reduce plastic waste?
  • How do I track my order after checkout?

Color Schema

#27ae60 (Green) – Used for headings and buttons to evoke sustainability and growth.

#16a085 (Teal) – Used for accents, links, and highlights throughout the site.

Typography

Arial, sans-serif – Used for body text for readability.

Georgia, serif – Used for headings to give a natural, trustworthy feel.

Testing

This page will be validated using the W3C HTML Validator and the W3C CSS Validator.

Additional checks will include accessibility (color contrast and alt text where appropriate), SEO best practices, and performance tests.
